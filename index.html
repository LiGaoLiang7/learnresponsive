About

That being said, it is a good thing to try to improve one's language and communication skills. Maybe we need to ask not how to emulate the native speakers but ask how to improve the language skills for specific purposes which you need to figure out based on your own situations.

Overall, I think the bottom line is that we all need to do everything we can to improve our verbal skills that are essential for effective communication in our professional and personal life. There is no need to feel inferior about being a non-native speaker of English. Ultimately the quality of your English really depends on how educated and informed you are over the topics you are speaking or writing about.
